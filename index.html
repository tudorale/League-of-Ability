About the Game

Welcome to League of Ability,
this minigame consists of guessing the abilities of the champions from the original League of Legends game,
an image with an ability of a certain champion will appear
and you have to press the "Answer" button and in the box it will appear you will have to enter the name of the champion,
if you guess you will go to the next level, press the 'Hint' button if you don't know the answer on first try.
