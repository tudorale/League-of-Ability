About the Game

League of Ability is a minigane which consists of guessing the abilities ofthe champions from the original League of Legends game, an image with an ability of a certain champion will appear and you have to press the "Answer" button and in the box it will appear you will have to enter the name of the champion, if you guess you will go to the next level, press the 'Hint' button if you don't know the answer on first try.
